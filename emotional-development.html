Reframe to return to

On difficult environments

You are passing through Lucent with intention, not something that is happening to you. You're there on purpose. You're taking specific things from it. You have an exit date. They don't get to define you.

On taking blame

Feeling something doesn't mean you caused it. Other people's deliberate choices about their behaviour are not a reflection of how you respond. The problem isn't your reaction, it's their actions.

On not being enough

You're 24. Amaea doesn't exist in formal sense yet. You have FCA depth, speaking reputation, Robin Powell, the CISI CEO, Intelliflo contacts. You're exactly where you should be.
