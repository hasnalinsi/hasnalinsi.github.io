Amaea

Expand within FCA, mortgages, insurance, investment

2031–32  ·  Age 30–31

First child

Target age 30–31. Build parental leave structure and business continuity plan well before this. A strong second-in-command makes this survivable as a founder.

2033–34  ·  Age 32–33

Strategic sale or partial exit. Retain equity and ongoing dividends. Exit groundwork starts 3–5 years before this point.
