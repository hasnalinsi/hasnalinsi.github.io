Amaea
Expand within FCA — mortgages, insurance, investment management
First expansion: adjacent FCA-regulated sectors. Same regulatory environment, same buyer profile, same credibility. Product adapts rather than rebuilds. This is the first multiplier on revenue.
2031–32 · Age 30–31
Personal
First child
Target age 30–31. Build parental leave structure and business continuity plan before this point. A strong second-in-command and documented operations make this survivable as a founder.
2033–34 · Age 32–33
Personal
Second child
Full FCA coverage by this point. Business should have an ops layer that doesn't require founder involvement in day-to-day — this is the critical structure to build before 2031.
2035–37 · Age 34–36
Amaea
Cross-sector or international expansion
Legal sector, EU/international regulatory markets. By this point Amaea is the dominant AI compliance tool for UK FCA-regulated firms — the expansion is a proven playbook applied to new sectors.
~2040 · Age ~39
Exit
Exit and retire as founder
Strategic sale or partial exit. Retain equity and ongoing dividends. Advisory role. Exit groundwork — clean financials, documented systems, auditable revenue — starts 3–5 years before this point.
